The Origin

Two gaps. One solution.

Willed Company Limited was conceived from a clear-eyed recognition of two persistent gaps in Kenya’s business environment: the consistent failure of supply chains to deliver quality medical and general goods reliably, and the scarcity of accessible professional financial advisory for thousands of SMEs, NGOs, and institutions.

Rather than address one problem or the other, Willed Company Limited was designed to solve both — creating a company that is simultaneously a trusted supplier and a strategic financial partner. This is the company’s core competitive advantage.
