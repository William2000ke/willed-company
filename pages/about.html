The Origin

Two gaps. One solution.

Willed Company Limited was conceived from a clear-eyed recognition of two persistent gaps in Kenya's business environment: first, the consistent failure of supply chains to deliver quality medical and general goods reliably to institutions outside major procurement hubs; and second, the scarcity of accessible, professional financial advisory services for the thousands of SMEs, NGOs, and institutions that operate without expert financial guidance.

Rather than address one problem or the other, Willed Company Limited was designed to solve both — creating a company that is simultaneously a trusted supplier and a strategic financial partner. This integration is not accidental. It is the company's core competitive advantage.

Founded by Edwin William Otieno and anchored in Nairobi with plans to serve all 47 counties, Willed Company Limited begins operations in 2026 with a three-year strategic plan, a formalised partnership structure, and an unshakeable commitment to doing business with integrity.

Our Edge

What makes us different.

01
Dual-Service Model
Supplies and financial advisory under one roof — a combination not offered by any competitor in the Kenyan market. Clients get more value from a single relationship.
02
Deep Kenyan Market Knowledge
We understand PPRA regulations, SHA procurement frameworks, county devolution dynamics, and KRA compliance — built into everything we do.
03
Flexible Credit Terms
We offer tailored payment terms for qualifying institutional clients — recognising that government and NGO procurement cycles don't always align with standard 30-day invoicing.
04
Technology-Enabled Operations
Digital inventory tracking, client order portals, and cloud-based financial reporting give every client real-time visibility into their supply and financial position.
05
Strategic Partnership Mindset
We don't chase transactions. We build long-term client relationships where our success is directly tied to theirs — a fundamentally different commercial model.
06
Ethical Foundation
Zero-compromise on transparency, product quality, and regulatory compliance. In a market where corners are often cut, our integrity is a measurable competitive advantage.
