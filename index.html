Who We Are

Built on vision,
purpose, and resolve.

Willed Company Limited was founded to close two critical gaps in Kenya's business landscape: the unreliable access to quality medical and general supplies, and the scarcity of professional financial guidance for institutions and growing businesses.

Our founding team combines expertise in procurement, logistics, healthcare supply chains, and financial management — delivering something genuinely unique: a company that supplies what you need today and helps you plan wisely for tomorrow.
